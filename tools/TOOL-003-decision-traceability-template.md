## Overview

The Decision Traceability Template is a governance tool designed to document and reconstruct decision processes within AI-mediated systems.

As AI systems increasingly participate in decision-making, organizations must be able to trace how decisions are formed, what inputs were used, and how human judgment influenced the final outcome.

This tool provides a structured template for recording decision flows in a consistent and auditable manner.

---

## Purpose

This tool enables organizations to:

• document decision processes involving AI systems  
• capture system inputs and outputs  
• record human judgment and intervention  
• reconstruct decision pathways for audit or review  
• improve transparency and accountability  

---

## Scope

This tool applies to:

• AI-assisted decision workflows  
• automated recommendation systems  
• operational decision processes  
• compliance-sensitive environments  
• high-risk or high-impact decisions  

---

## Core Concept

Decision traceability refers to the ability to reconstruct:

• what information was available  
• what the system produced  
• how humans interacted with the output  
• what decision was ultimately made  

The objective is not to log everything, but to capture key elements necessary for understanding and accountability.

---

## Decision Record Template

Each decision instance can be recorded using the following structure:

### Basic Information

• Decision ID  
• Date / Time  
• Context / Scenario  

---

### Input

• Data sources  
• Relevant signals or parameters  
• External conditions 

---
### System Output

• AI-generated recommendation or prediction  
• Confidence Level: Available when provided  
• Model or System Reference: Optional  

---

### Human Judgment

• Reviewer or Decision-Maker  
• Interpretation of System Output  
• Modifications: If applicable  
• Rationale for Decision  

---

#### Final Decision

• Decision Outcome  
• Action Taken  
• Execution Method: Automated or Manual  

---

#### Responsibility

• Responsible Entity  
• Role or Function  
• Approval: When Applicable  

---

#### Risk Assessment

• Risk Level: Low / Medium / High  
• Known Uncertainties  
• Potential Impact  

---

## Simplified Example

Decision ID: D-2026-001  
Context: Loan Approval  

Input: Applicant Financial Data  

System Output: Approval Recommended  
Confidence Level: 82%  

Human Judgment:

• Reviewed Income Stability  
• Adjusted Risk Perception  

Final Decision: Approved with Conditions  

Responsible Entity: Credit Manager  

Risk Level: Medium

## Implementation Guidance

Organizations should:

1. Define which decisions require traceability.

2. Standardize the decision recording format.

3. Ensure responsible parties are identified.

4. Store records in a retrievable and consistent manner.

5. Align traceability with governance and audit needs.

---

## Expected Outcomes

Using this tool enables organizations to:

• reconstruct decision processes  
• improve accountability and audit readiness  
• identify weaknesses in decision workflows  
• enhance transparency in AI-assisted decisions  
• support regulatory compliance  

---

## Limitations

This tool does not:

• require full data logging for every process  
• guarantee decision correctness  
• replace formal audit or compliance systems  

It provides a structured framework for decision documentation only.
